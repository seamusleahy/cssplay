Bug

In Mozilla 1.5a, when the sub-submenus first appear the links that are earlier in order in the HTML overlap the links that come after. If one moves their mouse over another link in the submenu and then back over the link that has the sub-submenu, the sub-submenu will now have the links that are later in the HTML overlap the links that are earlier.
