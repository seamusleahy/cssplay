Place your mouse over the blue bar on the side to se the navigation menu. An example of where to use this is to have the default stylesheet show the sidebar and then with the stylesheet changer create an auto-hide sidebar.

The only browser known to work with this page is Mozilla 1.3b or later. Something is buggy in Mozilla 1.4a. When the sidebar is fixed positioned it fails; but when it is absolute positioned it works fine. This can be checked out by switching to the alternate stylesheet title "Absolute."
